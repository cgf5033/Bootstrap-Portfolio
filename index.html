About Me

My name is Colin Fitzhenry and I am a 25 year living in Westfield, New Jersey. I graduated from Westfield High School in 2011 and went on to study industrial engineering at Penn State University.

Despite living in the New Jersey for more than 20 years, I am acually a Canadian citizen and I was born in Brussels, Belgium (I know, confusing). I have lived all over the world including London, Kalamazoo Michigan, Belgium, and Perth Australia.

My hobbies include listening to music, golfing, boxing, skiing, snowboarding, and watching my favorite sports teams the New Jersey Devils, New York Giants, New York Yankees and the Penn State Nittany Lions.
